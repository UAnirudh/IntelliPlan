Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect
IntelliPlan collects your email address and password (hashed and never stored in plain text) when you create an account. We also store your school login credentials (Canvas token, StudentVue username/password) encrypted in our database solely to fetch your assignments. We collect task completion data you voluntarily provide to improve scheduling predictions.
What we do not collect
We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties. We do not display advertising. We do not store your assignment content beyond what is needed to display it to you.
Third-party services
IntelliPlan uses Groq (AI scheduling), Google Calendar (optional, with your explicit permission), and Notion (optional, with your explicit permission). Each operates under their own privacy policy.
Data deletion
You can delete your account at any time from Settings. All associated data is permanently deleted immediately.

Questions? Join our Discord.
